# Instrument Sample Licenses

## Piano Samples

**Source:** University of Iowa Electronic Music Studios
**URL:** https://theremin.music.uiowa.edu/MISpiano.html
**License:** Free for any projects, without restrictions

The piano samples used in this project are from the University of Iowa Electronic Music Studios Musical Instrument Samples collection. Per their website:

> "The samples may be downloaded and used for any projects, without restrictions."

The samples were accessed via a GitHub mirror (https://github.com/xmacex/piano) which transcoded the original AIFF files to MP3 audio sprites.

### Samples Used
- Steinway Grand Piano, mezzo-forte (mf) dynamics
- Notes: C2, C3, C4, C5
- Format: MP3 audio sprite with timing offsets
