## What's IN REACH

### 1. Pitch Domain

| Capability | Range | Musical Significance |
|------------|-------|---------------------|
| **Chromatic notes** | 12 semitones per octave | Full Western chromatic scale |
| **Transposition** | ±12 semitones (2 octaves) | Can reach any key center |
| **Per-step pitch locks** | ±12 semitones | Melodic sequences, arpeggios |
| **Frequency range** | 40Hz - 8000Hz+ | Full audible bass to treble |

**Reachable scales (via careful step selection):**
- Major / Minor / Pentatonic / Blues
- Dorian / Phrygian / Lydian / Mixolydian / Locrian
- Harmonic minor / Melodic minor
- Whole tone / Diminished
- Any 12-TET scale

### 2. Rhythm Domain

| Capability | Range | Musical Significance |
|------------|-------|---------------------|
| **Step resolution** | 16th notes | Standard sequencer granularity |
| **Pattern length** | 4-64 steps | 1-4 bars per track |
| **Polyrhythm** | Independent step counts | 3:4, 4:5, etc. polyrhythms possible |
| **Swing** | 0-100% | Straight to heavy shuffle |
| **Tempo** | 60-180 BPM | Hip-hop through drum & bass |

**Reachable rhythmic feels:**
```
4/4 Standard     ✓ [x . . . x . . . x . . . x . . .]
Backbeat         ✓ [x . . . X . . . x . . . X . . .]
Off-beat hi-hat  ✓ [. x . x . x . x . x . x . x . x]
Syncopation      ✓ [x . . x . . x . . . x . . x . .]
Triplet feel     ✓ (via swing at ~66%)
Polyrhythm       ✓ (via different step counts per track)
```

## What's OUT OF REACH

### 1. Pitch Limitations

```
OUT OF REACH                          WHY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Microtonal intervals                  Chromatic grid is 12-TET only
(quarter tones, 19-TET, etc.)         No pitch resolution below semitone

Extended range (>2 octaves)           Transpose limited to ±12 semitones
                                      Can't reach extreme bass/treble

Pitch bend / portamento               No continuous pitch control
                                      Steps are discrete, not sliding

Vibrato / tremolo                     No LFO modulation on pitch
                                      Synths have fixed pitch per step

Detuning for thickness                Can't offset by cents, only semitones
```

**Musical styles affected:**
- Middle Eastern maqam (quarter tones)
- Indian classical (microtonal gamakas)
- Blues guitar bends
- Theremin-style glides
- Bass drops with pitch sweeps

### 2. Rhythm Limitations

```
OUT OF REACH                          WHY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Odd time signatures                   Fixed 4/4 assumption in UI
(5/4, 7/8, 9/8, 11/8)                Step counts: 4, 8, 16, 32, 64

True triplets                         No triplet subdivision option
                                      Only swing approximation

32nd notes / faster                   Minimum resolution is 16th notes
                                      No 32nd-note grid option

Metric modulation                     Tempo is fixed during playback
                                      No accelerando/ritardando

Rubato / expressive timing            Quantized grid only
                                      No humanization offset per note

Odd step counts (5, 7, 11, 13)        Only powers of 2 × options
                                      No prime number polymeters
```

**Musical styles affected:**
- Progressive rock (7/8, 5/4)
- Math rock (complex meters)
- Classical (rubato, ritardando)
- Jazz (behind/ahead of beat)
- Flamenco (12-beat compás)

### 3. Timbre Limitations

```
OUT OF REACH                          WHY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Acoustic instruments                  No sampled real instruments
(piano, guitar, strings, brass)       Only synthesized approximations

Vocal sounds                          No vocal synth or formant filter
                                      (can record mic samples as workaround)

Filter sweeps / automation            No per-step filter cutoff locks
                                      Filter is static per preset

Amplitude envelope per step           ADSR is preset-global, not per-step
                                      Can't vary attack/decay

Distortion / saturation               No distortion effects
                                      Clean synthesis only

Reverb / delay / effects              No effects chain
                                      Dry output only

FM / granular / wavetable             Only subtractive synthesis
                                      4 waveforms (sin/tri/saw/sqr)
```

**Musical styles affected:**
- Orchestral / cinematic (needs real instruments)
- Lo-fi hip-hop (needs vinyl/tape saturation)
- Dub (needs delay/reverb)
- Industrial (needs distortion)
- Ambient (needs reverb, granular)

### 4. Polyphony & Voice Limitations

```
OUT OF REACH                          WHY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Polyphonic synths                     SynthEngine is monophonic
(play chord with one synth)           One voice per synth preset

Legato / mono mode with glide         No portamento between notes
                                      Each step restarts oscillator

Voice allocation modes                No round-robin, no unison
                                      Fixed one-voice-per-track model

Arpeggiation                          No auto-arpeggiator
                                      Must program each note manually
```

### 5. Structure & Arrangement Limitations

```
OUT OF REACH                          WHY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Song sections (verse/chorus)          Single pattern loops forever
                                      No scene/pattern chaining

Pattern chains                        No A→B→A→C sequencing
                                      Only one 64-step loop

Automation lanes                      No parameter automation over time
                                      (volume, pan, filter, etc.)

Mixer / panning                       No stereo positioning
                                      Mono summed output

Dynamic range (crescendo)             No global volume automation
                                      Static mix only
```

## Technical Constraints Driving Limitations

| Constraint | Technical Reason | Design Choice |
|------------|------------------|---------------|
| 12-TET only | `playbackRate = 2^(semitones/12)` | Web Audio standard tuning |
| ±12 semitones | Hardcoded `MIN_TRANSPOSE/MAX_TRANSPOSE` | UI simplicity |
| 16th note minimum | `STEPS_PER_BEAT = 4` constant | Grid usability |
| Monophonic synths | `activeVoices` map (one per track) | Performance/simplicity |
| No effects | No effects chain in audio graph | Scope/latency |
| Fixed pattern length | `MAX_STEPS = 64` | Memory/sync simplicity |
| No automation | No per-step modulation in `ParameterLock` | Complexity reduction |
